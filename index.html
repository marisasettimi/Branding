01 — Brand Overview

Rev Whisper.

An intelligent revenue management platform that optimizes listings, drives pricing strategy, and maximizes returns for short-term rental owners.

Our Belief

We believe every short-term rental deserves smarter pricing. Not guesswork. Not gut feelings. Revenue driven by algorithms that never sleep.

The Story

Built for hosts who want more from their listings.

Rev Whisper exists because most short-term rental owners leave revenue on the table. We combine proprietary algorithms with a dedicated tech team to optimize every listing, every night, every market — turning data into dollars while you focus on your guests.

The brand reflects this philosophy: precise, intelligent, confident. No noise, no empty promises. Just results you can measure.

Typography Guidelines
Do
Use Lora at large display sizes (42px+) for maximum impact
Keep body text in DM Sans at 400 weight for readability
Maintain generous line height (1.4–1.8) for breathing room
Use negative letter-spacing (-1px to -2px) on headlines only
Let the type hierarchy do the work — avoid bold for emphasis
Don't
Don't use Lora below 20px — switch to DM Sans for small sizes
Don't use more than two type sizes on a single card or component
Don't set all-caps in Lora — it loses its warmth
Don't stretch, compress, or outline the letterforms
Don't mix in third-party typefaces without brand approval
03 — Color System

Less is the
whole point.

A restrained, intentional palette that conveys precision, authority, and sophistication through simplicity. We rely entirely on black and white.

Primary Palette

Four colors. Infinite confidence.

The Rev Whisper palette pairs a warm neutral foundation with a single, intentional green accent. We speak through contrast and restraint — one accent color is all we need.

Dune
#32302F
RGB 50, 48, 47
Primary brand color. Used for headlines, body text, navigation, and icons. Warm black — never use pure #000000.
Fern
#4A6741
RGB 74, 103, 65
Brand accent color. Used for the "Rev" wordmark, key call-to-actions, and selective emphasis. Use sparingly — its power comes from restraint.
Supporting Palette
White
#FFFFFF · RGB 255, 255, 255
Primary background. Generous white space is a defining feature of the brand. Let it breathe. Cards, sections, and call-to-actions follow this rule.
Stone
#E0DCDA · RGB 224, 220, 218
Dividers, borders, rule lines, and subtle backgrounds. Structural color for separating content areas without drawing attention.
Usage Distribution

White space is still the primary color.

The majority of any Rev Whisper design should be white. Dune enters for text and structure. Fern appears only at moments of emphasis — the logo, a button, a key data point. Never overuse it.

Color Philosophy
Restraint is confidence
A minimal palette signals that we don't need to shout. The brand speaks through typography and data, not color tricks.
Warm, never cold
Our dark isn't blue-black — it's a warm charcoal (#32302F). Our green isn't neon — it's an earthy, grounded fern (#4A6741). Everything leans warm.
Green earns its place
Fern is used for the "Rev" wordmark and strategic moments of emphasis. If everything is green, nothing is. Reserve it for what matters most.
Never use pure black
Dune (#32302F) replaces black everywhere in the brand. Pure #000000 feels harsh and digital. Dune feels considered and intentional.

Symbol Guidelines

Protect the integrity of the mark.

Do
Use the icon in approved brand colors only (Dune, Fern, or White)
Maintain the 25% clear space minimum around the icon at all times
Use the icon at 32px or larger to preserve waveform detail
Place the icon on solid, uncluttered backgrounds
Use the icon as a standalone mark for favicons, app icons, and social avatars
Don't
Don't change the stroke weight or modify the waveform shape
Don't apply gradients, shadows, outlines, or 3D effects to the icon
Don't rotate, skew, stretch, or distort the icon in any way
Don't place the icon on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds
Don't recreate or redraw the icon — always use the original SVG asset

Logo Guidelines

Rules for protecting the mark.

Do
Always use the approved logo files — never recreate the lockup manually
Maintain the fixed relationship between icon and wordmark
Use "Rev" in Fern (#4A6741) and "Whisper" in Dune (#32302F) on light backgrounds
Ensure the logo has sufficient contrast against its background
Scale the logo proportionally — never resize width or height independently
Don't
Don't separate the icon from the wordmark in the lockup arrangement
Don't change the color relationship — "Rev" is always the accent color
Don't place the icon on the right side or below the wordmark
Don't add effects like drop shadows, outlines, bevels, or gradients
Don't set the wordmark in a different typeface — it must be Lora
