Logo Guidelines

Rules for protecting the mark.

Do
Always use the approved logo files — never recreate the lockup manually
Maintain the fixed relationship between icon and wordmark
Use "Rev" in Fern (#4A6741) and "Whisper" in Dune (#32302F) on light backgrounds
Ensure the logo has sufficient contrast against its background
Scale the logo proportionally — never resize width or height independently
Don't
Don't separate the icon from the wordmark in the lockup arrangement
Don't change the color relationship — "Rev" is always the accent color
Don't place the icon on the right side or below the wordmark
Don't add effects like drop shadows, outlines, bevels, or gradients
Don't set the wordmark in a different typeface — it must be Lora
