Brand Icon

The Rev Whisper symbol.

The icon combines a speech bubble with an algorithmic waveform — representing intelligent, data-driven conversations about revenue. It serves as the standalone brand mark and pairs with the wordmark to form the full logo.

Icon Variants

Three approved versions.

The icon works in Dune, Fern, or reversed on dark backgrounds. No other color treatments are permitted.

Clear Space

Give the icon room to breathe.

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to 25% of the icon's height on all sides. No text, graphics, or other elements may enter this zone.

25% height

Minimum Sizing

Legibility at every scale.

The icon must remain recognizable at all sizes. Below these minimums, the waveform detail is lost and the mark becomes illegible.

64px — Large
48px — Standard

Symbol Guidelines

Protect the integrity of the mark.

Do
Use the icon in approved brand colors only (Dune, Fern, or White)
Maintain the 25% clear space minimum around the icon at all times
Use the icon at 32px or larger to preserve waveform detail
Place the icon on solid, uncluttered backgrounds
Use the icon as a standalone mark for favicons, app icons, and social avatars
Don't
Don't change the stroke weight or modify the waveform shape
Don't apply gradients, shadows, outlines, or 3D effects to the icon
Don't rotate, skew, stretch, or distort the icon in any way
Don't place the icon on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds
Don't recreate or redraw the icon — always use the original SVG asset
