Typography Guidelines
Do
Use Tiempos at large display sizes (42px+) for maximum impact
Keep body text in The Future at 400 weight for readability
Maintain generous line height (1.4–1.8) for breathing room
Use negative letter-spacing (-1px to -2px) on headlines only
Let the type hierarchy do the work — avoid bold for emphasis
Don't
Don't use Tiempos below 20px — switch to The Future for small sizes
Don't use more than two type sizes on a single card or component
Don't set all-caps in Tiempos — it loses its warmth
Don't stretch, compress, or outline the letterforms
Don't mix in third-party typefaces without brand approval
